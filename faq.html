FAQ

Last updated: May 11, 2015
We will keep updating the most frequently asked questions.


Login
Why do I have to log in before adding items to cart?
Because we want to make sure you have a good shopping experience on our website. When you click the add to cart button, the system will keep the item for you for the next 24 hours even if that is the last one. If we don’t do this , other people could buy the last one because they check out first even though they added the item to their cart later than you. That will be very frustrating. We want to see to it that you have the item you want temporaliy, even if you decide to remove the item at the last minute.


Payment
Can I process to checkout if I don’t have a paypal account?
Yes , you can . We use PayPal Website Payments Standard to accept debit cards, credit cards and bank account payments - all through one provider. We keep looking for better solutions to allow you to pay on our website.


Shipping
What countries do you ship to? Do you ship to my country?
We ship most of our products around the world via UPS. The cost of shipping you have to pay depends on your total purchase amount and your country. Please see thedelivery and returnsto get the latest information.


What’s the total cost when shopping on the website?
We are sorry that we can only calculate the item price and shipping cost for you. You might be charged customs or import duties in your country. Because customs policies is widely different in each country, we have no list of charges and cannot inform you what they will be. If you have any questions regarding this, please contact your local customs office before placing the orders.


How do I cancel my order?
You can cancel your order, if the order has not been sent from our warehouse in Taiwan. Please send us an e-mail atservice@naknakdesign.com


Returns
What do I do to return products?
If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, please contact us before you send the product:
- By email: service@naknakdesign.com


Our products can be returned within 15 days of the original purchase of the product.


To be eligible for a return, please make sure that:


- The product was purchased in the last 15 days
- The product is in its original packaging   
- The product isn't used or damaged


Products that do not meet these criteria will not be considered for return.
