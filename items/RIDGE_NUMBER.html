RIDGE NUMBER  black_0

RIDGE NUMBER is a numeral signage system for both outdoor and indoor spaces. In a casting process, a draft angle is needed to allow for a smoother mold-release. The ridge design is inspired by the inevitable draft angle and exaggerated it to the most. The subtle changes in the shading on RIDGE NUMBER when light is casted from different directions makes your spaces more unique.
