HOOKS  6_red

HOOKS is a family of functionally decorative hangers. The shape came from the play of repeating a small hook. It is small, but its many hooks offer a lot of organizing options before it is full. Objects ranging from keys to clothes to coats can be hung on the versatile hooks. When empty, it becomes an ornament reminiscent of a flower on the wall.
